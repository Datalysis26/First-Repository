# Mathematical Implementations & Quantitative Models

Welcome! This repository serves as a centralized hub for my implementations across various mathematical disciplines. My focus is on translating theoretical mathematical frameworks into efficient, clean, and scalable code, spanning fields from quantitative finance to numerical analysis.

---

## 🌌 Project Taxonomy

### 📈 Quantitative Finance & Financial Engineering
*   **Derivatives Pricing:** Monte Carlo simulations for exotic options.

### 🔬 Numerical Analysis & Scientific Computing
*   **Linear Algebra:** Custom implementations of matrix decompositions ($LU$, $QR$, $SVD$) and iterative solvers.

### ⚙️ Mathematical Optimization & Operations Research
*   **Non-Linear Optimization:** Quasi-Newton methods (BFGS).

### 📊 Data Science / Analysis & Mathematical Statistics
*   **Stochastic Processes:** Brownian motion simulations.
*   **Machine Learning:** Logistic regression.
*   **Data analysis:** SQL-queries on real statistics.
